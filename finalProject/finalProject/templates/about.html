About the System

The Santos College Staff Management System is built to simplify daily administrative tasks — from tracking attendance and managing leave requests to organizing staff profiles and payroll. Designed for educational institutions, it helps streamline HR processes with ease, efficiency, and reliability.

Organize staff information and departments.

Developed by Information Systems students of Santos College — a practical project that transforms HR tasks into a smart solution.
